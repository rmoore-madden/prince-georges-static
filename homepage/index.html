Winter Times Are Here!

Prince George’s County is shining bright this winter with businesses around the county offering an array of activities and events for all to enjoy. There are drive-thru adventures, icy activities, one-of-a-kind horseback riding and carriage tours, specialty foods and drinks, lively entertainment, great shopping, and more!

Book a winter getaway for you and your family. Get cozy with a mug of hot cocoa. Visit one of our farms for refreshing treats. Experience our museums for a look into the past. It’s a wintery time to be in Prince George’s.

We hope to see you soon.
