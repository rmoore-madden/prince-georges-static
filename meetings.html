Meetings & Conventions

Affordable Meeting & Event Venues

Our unique destination showcases over 12,000 guest rooms and offers over 850,000 square feet of meeting & exhibit space. Prince George’s County hosts exquisite convention and meeting venues including two Four-Diamond hotels — the Gaylord National Resort & Convention Center , which includes a 180,000 square foot exhibit hall, and the MGM National Harbor Resort & Casino.

Additional options include The HOTEL at University of Maryland, the College Park Marriott Hotel & Conference Center, Crowne Plaza College Park, Harborside Hotel and many more.

Looking for something rare or cutting edge? Consider the contemporary Flight Deck at the Capital Wheel or FedEx Field. Need a more intimate space? Check out our unique venues and historical sites like the Newton White Mansion or Oxon Hill Manor to name a few.

One of the area’s hottest attractions is the National Harbor, situated on the banks of the historic Potomac River. Voted one of the Top 50 of US Destinations for meetings, this lively hub has incredible views of the nation’scapital, creating a perfect backdrop for special events, meetings or conventions. When the meetings are over, attendees can explore the surrounding waterfront district featuring boutique shops, restaurants, water activities and world-class entertainment options in a fun and safe walkable area.

Accessible Location

Located just outside downtown Washington, DC, Prince George’s County is easily accessible from three surrounding airports — Reagan National, Baltimore/Washington International and Dulles International. Amtrak and the Metro Transit are readily available and make getting around a breeze with conveniently located stops in and around the city.

Authentic Amenities & Area Attractions

Free time is meant for fun, and the fun is limitless with the nearby casino, breweries and entertainment venues like TopGolf. Stretch your legs touring our Legacy Wine Trail or playing a round on one of our golf courses. Visit working farms, historical landmarks, a wide variety of art districts, museums, great shopping and delicious dining options.
